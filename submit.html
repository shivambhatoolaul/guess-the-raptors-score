1. Since you guessed the outcome correctly (W, in this case), you start at +100 points. Wrong guesses start at +75 points instead.

2. Next, we substract the sum of the absolute differences (abs) between the scores. In our case, that would be abs(130-120) + abs(90-100) = 20.

So, your final score for Game 1 would be +100 points-20 points = 80 points.
