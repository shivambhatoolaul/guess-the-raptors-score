SCORING SYSTEM?

Let's say that the final score for Game 1 was 130-90, but you guessed that the score was going to be 120-100.

Since you guessed the outcome correctly (W, in this case), you start at +100 points. Wrong guesses start at +75 points instead.

From there you lose points depending on the actual score vs the score you guessed.

We calculate this by adding the differences of the absolute values (abs) between the scores. In our case, we would do abs(130-120) + abs(90-100) = 20.

So, our final score for Game 1 would be +100 points-20 points = 80 points.

The player with the most points at the end of the 8 games wins.
